Assalamualaikum

The beautiful Islamic greeting of peace.

MeaningImportanceFAQ

Meaning

السلام عليكم means Peace be upon you. It is a prayer asking Allah to grant peace, mercy and blessings.

Complete greeting:
السلام عليكم ورحمة الله وبركاته

Response:
وعليكم السلام ورحمة الله وبركاته

Importance in Islam

Muslims greet one another with Assalamualaikum to spread peace, strengthen brotherhood and follow the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. This greeting reminds believers to begin every interaction with kindness and respect.

Benefits include strengthening relationships, earning reward, encouraging good manners, and creating a welcoming atmosphere in homes, mosques and communities.
